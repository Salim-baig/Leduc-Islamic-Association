Ways to Give

Choose how you'd like to support the community

General Donation

Support the overall operations and maintenance of the association, including utilities, supplies, and day-to-day running costs.

Education Fund

Help fund Islamic education programs for children and adults, including Quran classes, weekend school, and educational materials.

Building Fund

Contribute to the growth and development of our facilities, helping us expand to better serve the growing community.

Ramadan Fund

Support our Ramadan programs including daily iftars, taraweeh prayers, and special Ramadan educational sessions.

Zakat

Fulfill your Zakat obligation through the association. Funds are distributed to eligible recipients in accordance with Islamic guidelines.

Sadaqah

Give voluntary charity (sadaqah) to support community members in need and fund community outreach programs.
