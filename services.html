Our Services

Programs and services for the community

Worship Services

Daily and weekly prayer services

Daily Prayers

Five daily congregational prayers in a welcoming and spiritual environment. Join us for Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha prayers.

Friday Jumu'ah

Weekly Friday congregational prayer and sermon (khutbah) at 1:30 PM. An essential gathering for the Muslim community.

Eid Celebrations

Community gatherings for Eid ul-Fitr and Eid ul-Adha celebrations including prayer, festivities, and shared meals.

Education Programs

Learning opportunities for all ages

Quran Classes

Weekly Quran recitation, tajweed, and memorization classes for children and adults. Learn to read and understand the Holy Quran with proper pronunciation.

Islamic Studies

Educational programs covering Islamic history, theology, jurisprudence (fiqh), and the life of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ.

Weekend Islamic School

Sunday school program for children covering Quran, Arabic, Islamic manners, and the foundations of faith. Every Sunday from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM.

Arabic Language

Arabic language classes to help community members understand the Quran and prayers in their original language.

Community Services

Supporting our community members

Youth Programs

Engaging activities, sports, mentorship programs, and leadership development for the youth of our community.

Family Counselling

Guidance and support for families on matters of faith, relationships, and navigating life's challenges from an Islamic perspective.

New Muslim Support

Resources, mentorship, and community support for new Muslims as they begin their journey in Islam.

Nikah Services

Islamic marriage ceremony services conducted by our Imam, including pre-marital counselling and documentation.

Janazah Services

Funeral prayer and burial assistance for community members, including guidance on Islamic funeral rites.

Community Meals

Regular community iftars, potlucks, and shared meals that bring families together and strengthen bonds.

Get Involved

We welcome volunteers and community members to help with our programs and services.
