About Our Community

The Leduc Islamic Association serves as a spiritual home for Muslims in Leduc and surrounding areas. We are dedicated to fostering a vibrant, inclusive, and welcoming community rooted in the teachings of Islam.

Our mission is to provide a place of worship, education, and community engagement where individuals and families can grow in faith, knowledge, and service to others.

Community

Building strong bonds through shared faith and mutual support

Education

Providing Islamic education for all ages and backgrounds

Service

Serving our neighbours and contributing to the wider community

Our Services

Daily Prayers

Weekly Quran recitation and memorization classes for children and adults.

Islamic Studies

Educational programs covering Islamic history, theology, and jurisprudence.

Youth Programs

Engaging activities and mentorship programs for the youth of our community.

Eid Celebrations
