Our Community

The Leduc Islamic Association serves as a spiritual home for Muslims in Leduc and surrounding areas. We are dedicated to fostering a vibrant, inclusive, and welcoming community rooted in the teachings of Islam.

Our mission is to provide a place of worship, education, and community engagement where individuals and families can grow in faith, knowledge, and service to others.

Founded to meet the growing needs of the Muslim community in Leduc, our association has become a cornerstone for spiritual growth, cultural preservation, and community building in the region.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a welcoming space for worship, education, and community engagement that nurtures the spiritual, social, and intellectual growth of Muslims in Leduc and the surrounding areas.

Our Vision

To be a thriving Islamic centre that serves as a beacon of faith, knowledge, and compassion — building bridges within the Muslim community and with our neighbours in the wider Leduc community.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Community

Building strong bonds through shared faith and mutual support, creating a family atmosphere where everyone belongs.

Education

Providing Islamic education for all ages and backgrounds, nurturing a love for learning and understanding of our faith.

Service

Serving our neighbours and contributing to the wider community through charitable works and outreach programs.

Inclusivity

Welcoming all Muslims regardless of background, ethnicity, or school of thought in a spirit of unity and brotherhood.

Integrity

Operating with transparency, accountability, and adherence to Islamic principles in all our endeavours.

Growth

Continuously striving to improve our programs, facilities, and impact for the benefit of current and future generations.
